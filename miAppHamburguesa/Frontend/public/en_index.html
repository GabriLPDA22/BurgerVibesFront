Our Concept

BurgerVibes is a burger joint in Zaragoza that was born from the friendship and passion for food shared by Alfonso and Gabriel. The goal is clear: to elevate the burger experience to another level by using creative recipes and the finest ingredients available.

At BurgerVibes, each burger tells a unique story. Inspired by their shared love for food, Alfonso and Gabriel have created a space where customers can enjoy authentic flavors and unforgettable moments.

The decor of the place reflects the personality and entrepreneurial spirit of the founders, with details that make each visit special.

Undoubtedly, BurgerVibes is the place where burgers come to life and friendships are strengthened.
